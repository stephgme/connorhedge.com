ABOUT

Music is more than a hobby or career for me. It’s a way of living. It infiltrates every part of my being from the minute I wake up in the morning. I have been blessed to travel all over the states and work with some amazing artists and musicians over the years. From performing in the studio to mixing and editing, I love every part of modern music production.

Born in Portland, OR, music was introduced to me at a young age. My mother and father are both musicians and music-lovers and raised me to play instruments and love music for myself. From Michael Jackson to Count Basie, I was raised on all kinds of music. These influences have shaped who I am and what I draw from when creating.
