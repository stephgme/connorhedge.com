My Work

ABOUT

Born an Oregonian, music was introduced to me at a young age. My mother and father are both musicians and music-lovers and raised me to play instruments and love music for myself. From Michael Jackson to Count Basie to Minor Threat—I grew up within the vast world of music. Artists like these have continued to shape and inspire the way I create music.

Music is much more than just a hobby or career. It is a way of living and a way to make meaning. It captivates every part of my being. I can’t help but curate songs, or hear beats and melodies everywhere I go from the minute I wake up to when I fall asleep. For the past ten years, I have been blessed to travel all over the country working alongside amazing artists and musicians. Whether it's performing live, writing in the studio, mixing or editing—I love every piece of music production.
